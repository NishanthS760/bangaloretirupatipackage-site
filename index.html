Your one-day pilgrimage timeline

From the liveable 2:30 AM check-in to the joyful prasadam on the ride home.

  1. Pickup & departure

    Board at Majestic, Indiranagar, or KR Puram. AC sleeper with charging ports and curated devotional playlist sets the tone.

  2. Comfortable night journey

    Rest on clean sleeper berths. Blankets provided, midnight tea break optional (our driver’s masala chai is famous).

  3. Freshen up on arrival

    Private rooms in Tirupati with hot water, essentials, and light refreshments before heading uphill.

  4. TTD Special Entry Darshan

    Guided entry for Balaji and Padmavathi darshan. We handle ID checks, laddus, and crowd updates in real time.

  5. Breakfast & lunch

    Authentic South Indian breakfast, and a hearty Andhra lunch post-darshan. Jain/fasting meals on prior request.

  6. Same-day return

    Depart Tirupati post-lunch, reach Bangalore between 10:00–11:30 PM with drops at original pickup points.

Typical timings: Check-in 2:30–3:00 AM • Darshan 8:30–11:00 AM • Lunch 1:00 PM • Return 10:00–11:30 PM. Crowd conditions may adjust these slightly.

Frequently asked questions

If your question isn’t here, call or WhatsApp us – we love helping pilgrims plan.

What exactly is included in the package?

AC sleeper bus, private freshen-up room, breakfast, lunch, prasadam, verified guide, official TTD Special Entry assistance, and same-day return to Bangalore.

Is the TTD ₹300 Special Entry ticket included in the price?

We handle the booking and documentation, but the ₹300 ticket amount is paid directly to TTD in your name.

What is the cancellation policy?

100% refund 48+ hours before departure, 50% refund between 24–48 hours, and no refund for cancellations within 24 hours of departure.

Can seniors or kids travel comfortably?

Yes. Sleeper berths are padded, we schedule comfort halts, and our guides help senior citizens through the darshan process. Share special needs during booking so we can plan ahead.
