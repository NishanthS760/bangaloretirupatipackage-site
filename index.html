Your Sacred Journey

A seamless one-day pilgrimage, thoughtfully planned for spiritual fulfillment

  1. Early Morning Pickup

    Comfortable pickup from convenient Bangalore locations: Majestic, Indiranagar, KR Puram, Electronic City. Travel in luxury AC sleeper bus with charging ports, clean blankets, and individual berths for restful journey.

  2. Night Journey to Tirupati

    Relax and prepare spiritually during the 4-hour journey. Our experienced drivers ensure safe, comfortable travel through scenic routes. Light snacks available onboard.

  3. Private Freshen-Up Facilities

    Arrive refreshed with exclusive access to clean, private freshen-up rooms near Tirupati. Hot water, towels, and changing facilities provided for your comfort.

  4. Official TTD Special Entry Darshan

    Priority darshan through official TTD Special Entry (₹300 paid to TTD). Our verified guide accompanies you through Balaji Temple and Padmavathi Temple, explaining rituals and significance. Complete spiritual experience without hassle.

  5. Authentic South Indian Meals

    Savor traditional South Indian breakfast and authentic Andhra-style vegetarian lunch at reputed local restaurants. Pure sattvic meals prepared with devotion.

  6. Return Journey & Drop

    Same-day return to Bangalore with drop at original pickup points. Arrive home by late evening, carrying divine blessings and cherished memories of your pilgrimage.

What documents are required for TTD Special Entry Darshan?

Government-issued photo identification is mandatory for TTD Special Entry:

  • Adults (12+ years): Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving License, or PAN Card
  • Children (5-12 years): Birth Certificate, School ID, or Aadhaar Card
  • Children (below 5 years): No ID required (free entry)

Important:

  • Original documents required - photocopies not accepted
  • All family members need individual IDs
  • Documents verified at TTD counters before darshan
  • We provide complete documentation guidance during booking
What should I wear for Tirupati Balaji darshan?

TTD enforces a strict traditional dress code for darshan:

For Men:

  • Dhoti, pyjama, or pants with shirt/kurta
  • Traditional veshti (dhoti) preferred
  • No shorts, T-shirts, or sleeveless clothing

For Women:

  • Saree, half-saree, or salwar kameez with dupatta
  • Churidar with dupatta acceptable
  • No sleeveless tops or revealing western wear

Additional Notes:

  • We provide guidance on appropriate attire during booking
  • TTD provides free traditional clothing at darshan queues if needed
  • Remove footwear before entering temple premises
  • Cell phones and electronics not allowed inside temple
